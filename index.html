ABOUT

I’m a designer and frontend developer who loves creating clean, modern, and smooth digital experiences. I work mainly with HTML, CSS, Tailwind, and JavaScript, and I enjoy turning ideas into visuals and interactions that feel nice to use. I care a lot about small details—spacing, balance, colors, animations—because I think those are the things that make a website feel good. I like designing layouts that are simple but not boring, and coding them in a way that’s efficient and easy to maintain. Whether it’s a landing page, a personal website, or a small web app, I try to make everything look neat and clear. I’m always experimenting, learning new things, and improving my workflow as I go. If you’re into clean design, minimalism, and smooth UI—then we’ll get along just fine.
